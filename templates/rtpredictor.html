Sources

The main sources of PM 2.5 are:

  • Vehicles
  • Power Plants
  • Deforestation (Forest Fires, Wood Burning, Agricultural Burning)
  • Environmental Disasters such as Volcanic Eruption

Effects

The fine particles stay in the air and are inhaled by humans which enter the nose or throat and reach down into the lungs and circulatory system.
